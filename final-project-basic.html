Night sky captivates me. A vast stretch of canvas smeared with dark colors with soft clouds and twinkling dots of stars. Looking at the sky does not only fascinates one, but also brings a sense of humility. How tiny we are under this enormous sky. Yet how lucky we are to able to witness this dark beauty. We can only look up in wonderment and be grateful to be blessed with eyes that could capture its beauty every night.
